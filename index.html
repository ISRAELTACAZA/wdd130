La Paz, Bolivia

La Paz, Bolivia, is the world's highest administrative capital, dramatically situated in a deep canyon within the Andes Mountains, surrounded by snow-capped peaks like Illimani. It's a vibrant, cosmopolitan city characterized by steep hills, deep valleys, and a unique blend of Spanish colonial and indigenous Aymara cultures. Visitors can explore lively street markets, historical sites, and a striking natural landscape, though the high altitude requires acclimatization.
